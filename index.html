One-time setup: macOS blocks unsigned apps. Fix in 30 seconds:
  1. Download and unzip Chill (move to Applications if you want)
  2. Open Terminal and run:
    xattr -cr /Applications/Chill.app If in Downloads: xattr -cr ~/Downloads/Chill.app
  3. Open Chill normally - works forever after this!
The app is open source and safe. This is required for all free Mac apps without Apple's $99/year certificate.
